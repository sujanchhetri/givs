About GIVS Nepal

Guest Intern Volunteer Service offers a wide range of Internship and Volunteer programs for international Medical students Nepal. We cut out unnecessary costs to provide our students the most affordable and best experience of program/project. Unlike other organizations, the program criteria mainly depend on the interest/schedule of the participant. Participant (Internship program) needs to submit the details regarding his/her program of interest/curriculum at least two months before the program date along with a letter of confirmation emailed from the university to the organization.

Internship programs are planned for University students, graduates and career developers who want to explore their interests and develop professional skills and competencies. Furthermore, Volunteer programs are generally considered as a selfless activity/service who donates his/her time and effort to help the people in need. As mentioned above, volunteers are able to work on their chosen field of interest. They require to submit a brief report of their interest on the respected field of work at least two months before.

Our services are targeted at providing quality work in a safe environment. On of our staffs will be always attentive to the needs of the interns/volunteers throughout their session with GIVS in Nepal.

A sense of justice and a sense of fairness are the two main factors to which our organization adheres to.
