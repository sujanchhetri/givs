Join GIVS Nepal

GIVS offers Volunteer Program and Internship Program for International Medical Students/Professional in regard to the program schedule of the applicant. GIVS make available the programs to their applicants in the field of M.B.B.S, Dental, Nursing, Opthalmology.

FREE REGISTRATION

In contrast to other organizations GIVS offers the applicants to register free for their respective program/project.

ACCOMODATION

Campaign/Outreach: The students/participants will be provided to accommodate in the Home stay in remote areas. Otherwise, students could also stay in the nearby hotels. It requires transportation to reach the location. In the remaining cases the students/Participants will be offered to accommodate in the hotels. A combination of both home stay and hotels cannot be arranged. The students/participants are requested to inform about their interest of staying at least a month or two before the program date. The accurate sum covered for accommodation, food and transport, particularly depends on the type of hotel, type of food and also the distance covered.

MODE OF PAYMENT

The payment could be done in two installment period.
  • 50% Payment (a month before the program via bank transfer)
  • 50% Payment (a day before the date of the program)

Note: Registration should be at least two to three months before the start of the program. The registration is absolutely free of charge. The member/participant registration will be cancelled if he fails to pay the first term of the installment after one month of registration.

Included

  • Airport pickup
  • Training/Orientation
  • Accomodation
  • Food
  • Transport to program/project sites
  • Necessary material for program/project

Not Included

  • Flight, Insurance & Visa
  • Non work related transportation
  • Beverages and other personal purchases

Documents / Information Required

  • It is required to forward a copy of an authorized letter from the university to our email at least one to two months before the date of the session.
  • A copy of current academic credentials.
  • Two recent passport size photos
  • Copy of a valid passport
  • Mention of Dietary restrictions/allergies (if required).
  • Details of medical condition (if required)

SUPERVISION AND ASSISTANCE

  • Interns receive ongoing supervision, support and guidance under professionalized medical team and an on-site intern supervisor. In addition, Students will have 24/7 access to our staff at the location during outreach. Yet, our staff maintains a 24/7 mobile phone to answer at any time. The provision of an interpreter at the location.
  • Current academic credentials, Identification card of College, garments and personals.
  • We recommend our interns to communicate with our representative either by emails or phone calls to give feedback at convenience.

Discount for Team/Group

Guest Intern Volunteer Service offers a great discount to its students/participants who wish to start their session in batches.

Note: Group/Batch includes 5 students.

  • 5% discount will be provided with a group of 5 students.
  • 10% discount will be provided with a group more than 5 students.
